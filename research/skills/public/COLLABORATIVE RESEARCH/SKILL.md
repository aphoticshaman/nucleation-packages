## 7. ANTI-PATTERNS

### 7.1 Praise Spiral
**Symptom:** Mutual admiration replaces adversarial rigor
**Result:** Overconfident claims, blind spots, wasted cycles
**Fix:** Praise ban. Friction only. Celebrate kills not confirmations.

### 7.2 Sunk Cost Commitment
**Symptom:** Refusing to kill hypothesis with invested effort
**Result:** Bad ideas survive, good ideas starved of resources
**Fix:** Separate hypothesis authors from hypothesis killers

### 7.3 Complexity Worship
**Symptom:** Preferring elegant complex solutions over simple working ones
**Result:** 17% bleed beats fractal plasma lungs
**Fix:** "Kill the dream" protocol â€” murder beautiful ideas early

### 7.4 Emission Theater
**Symptom:** Generating PDFs/deliverables without validation
**Result:** False confidence, wasted downstream effort
**Fix:** No-bullshit emission rule â€” truth only

### 7.5 Serial Monologue
**Symptom:** Long AI emissions without human checkpoints
**Result:** Drift from intent, wasted tokens, lost kill opportunities
**Fix:** Checkpoint every major claim, human confirms or kills
