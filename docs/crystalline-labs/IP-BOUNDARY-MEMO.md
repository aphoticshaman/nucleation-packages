This document clarifies the scope of intellectual property owned by Crystalline Labs LLC versus intellectual property owned personally by the founder.

---

## 1. Company-Owned Intellectual Property

Crystalline Labs LLC owns all intellectual property created in the course of company activities, including but not limited to:

* Software architectures and systems developed for company products
* Deterministic analytic pipelines, rule engines, and governance mechanisms
* Evidence fusion, replayability, and auditability systems
* Documentation, designs, and implementations related to company products (including LatticeForge)
* Patent filings and trade secrets derived from the above

All such work is considered company work product.

---

## 2. Excluded Personal Research

The founder conducts independent personal research activities that are **not** part of Crystalline Labs LLC work product.

These include, but are not limited to:

* AIMO-3
* Kaggle competitions
* Personal experimental models, benchmarks, or exploratory research

Excluded personal research:

* Is conducted independently
* Does not use Crystalline Labs proprietary systems or confidential information
* Is not assigned to Crystalline Labs LLC
* Is not relied upon in company patent filings

---

## 3. Separation of Derivation

Company intellectual property is independently developed within Crystalline Labs LLC and does not derive from excluded personal research activities.

Where conceptual overlap exists at a high level, company implementations are independently designed, documented, and validated as original company work.

---

## 4. Purpose of This Statement

This document exists to:

* Clarify ownership boundaries
* Prevent ambiguity in future IP review or diligence
* Support clean patent prosecution
* Enable future hiring and acquisition without IP disputes
