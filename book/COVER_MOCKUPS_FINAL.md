## Production Files Needed

### For Kindle eBook
- **Dimensions:** 2560 × 1600 px (1.6:1 ratio)
- **Format:** JPEG or TIFF, RGB
- **File size:** Under 50MB

### Quick Canva/Photoshop Instructions

**Step 1:** Create 2560 × 1600 canvas

**Step 2:** Place art file, scale to cover (may need to crop/extend)

**Step 3:** Add gradient overlay at top/bottom for text legibility
- Top: linear-gradient(rgba(0,0,0,0.4), transparent)
- Bottom: linear-gradient(transparent, rgba(0,0,0,0.6))

**Step 4:** Add text per specs above

**Step 5:** Export as JPEG, quality 90%
